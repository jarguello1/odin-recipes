Gallo Pinto

Description

Nicaraguan Gallo Pinto is white rice mixed with kidney/red beans

Ingredients

  • Cooked Red/Kidney Beans
  • Cooked White Rice
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Cook sliced onions in a pan with canola oil
  2. Add rice to pan
  3. Add cooked red beans with water that the beans were cooked in.
  4. Cook ingredients together until heated through.
